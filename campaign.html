ROU Insurance Inc./
Ad Campaign

Each advertisement illustrates a life event which can be covered by the insurance. The events are shown through objects that disappeared from the paper due to crumpling, burning and tearing.

Most of the time we see peaceful images of happy families in insurance ad campaigns. In contrast to this there are provocative and minimalistic images that draw attention and help the message come across clearly and effectively.

Yellow is a bright and eye-catching color that helps advertisements to stand out among other ads. At the same time yellow is associated with happiness, that helps to create a contrast between frightning life events and positivity of having the insurance.
